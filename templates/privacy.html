Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data — basic information such as login timestamps, used solely to maintain your session.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate you and keep your session secure.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers located in India. We apply reasonable technical safeguards — including password hashing and access controls — to protect it from unauthorised access.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces for the interface. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. No personal data is sold or transferred to third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to request data deletion, please reach out through the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
